{% blocktranslate trimmed %} Terminklick is an online platform that enables businesses to manage and accept appointments from their customers. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. It applies to business accounts that use our platform and to customers who book appointments through it. Terminklick is operated from Switzerland and primarily serves customers in Switzerland; this policy is therefore governed by the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP). Where relevant, we also reference the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) below. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "2. Data We Collect" %}

{% translate "We collect the following categories of personal data:" %}

  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Business account data: business name, email address, postal address, phone number, and website URL provided at registration or in account settings.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Customer booking data: first name, last name, email address, phone number, and any optional message provided when booking an appointment.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Appointment details: date, time, service type, and assigned staff member for each booking.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Technical data: IP address, browser type, and request timestamps recorded in server access logs.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Session data: a session identifier stored in a strictly necessary cookie to keep you logged in.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "3. Purpose and Legal Basis" %}

{% translate "We process personal data for the following purposes:" %}

  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Providing the booking service — to create, confirm, and manage appointments between businesses and their customers. Legal basis: performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Sending transactional notifications — confirmation, cancellation, and reminder emails sent to customers and businesses. Legal basis: performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Account management — to operate business accounts, authenticate users, and provide access to the dashboard. Legal basis: performance of a contract (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Security and fraud prevention — to detect and prevent abuse, rate-limit requests, and maintain the integrity of the platform. Legal basis: legitimate interests (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "We do not process personal data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the appointment management service." %}

{% translate "4. Data Sharing and Third Parties" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} We share personal data only with the following categories of service providers, solely to the extent necessary to operate the platform: {% endblocktranslate %}

  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Email delivery provider: used to send booking confirmation and notification emails. Your name and email address are transmitted to deliver these messages.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Hosting provider: the servers on which this platform runs. All data stored on the platform resides on these servers.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "We do not sell personal data to any third party. We do not share data with advertising networks or analytics providers." %}

{% translate "5. International Transfers" %}

{% blocktranslate trimmed %} Where personal data is transferred to service providers outside the European Economic Area, we ensure an appropriate level of protection is in place through Standard Contractual Clauses or an applicable adequacy decision. Contact us at the address above for details of the specific safeguards applied. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "6. Retention" %}

  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Customer booking PII (name, email, phone, message): automatically anonymised 30 days after the appointment takes place. The booking record itself (date, time, service) is retained for business accounting purposes.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Business account data: retained for as long as the account is active. Deleted within 7 days of a verified account deletion request.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Server access logs (IP address, user-agent, timestamps): retained for 30 days, then deleted automatically.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "7. Cookies" %}

{% translate "We use the following cookies:" %}

  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}Session cookie (sessionid): strictly necessary. Keeps business users logged in to the dashboard. Expires at the end of the browser session or after 2 weeks, whichever comes first.{% endblocktranslate %}
  • {% blocktranslate trimmed %}CSRF cookie (csrftoken): strictly necessary. Protects forms against cross-site request forgery attacks. Not used for tracking.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% translate "We do not use analytics, advertising, or any non-essential cookies." %}
